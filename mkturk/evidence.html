Labeling instructions (Click to expand)
The task here is to annotate tweets in relation to a topic where you have to annotate what kind of evidence a tweet contains if any.
Evidence is a statement used to support or attack a topic or claim. This can be done in combination with a claim, or it can also be self contained if it is just stating facts or referencing studies related to the topic. If the evidence is not related to the discussed topic, it is marked as unrelated. There excits different types of evidence, and if a tweet contains any evidence should have the type of it annotated. If more than one type of evidence exists choose the type you think best describes the evidence in the tweet overall

Types of evidence Guidance
Anecdotal A description of an episode(s), centered on individual(s) or clearly located in place and/or in time.
Expert Testimony by a person, group, committee, organization with some known expertise / authority on the topic.
Study Results of a quantitative analysis of data, given as numbers, or as conclusions
Fact A known piece of information about the world without a clear source for the information
Normative An added description for a belief about the world
Unrelated The tweet does contain evidence, but it is not related to the topic
No Evidence The tweet does not contain a claim at all. Any tweets where none of the above classes fit should be marked as no claim
